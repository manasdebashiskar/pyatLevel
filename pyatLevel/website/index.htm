ORBCOMM Mobile Messaging Service
Pyat Level, a Content Provider through the Blackberry Alliance Program, manages the ORBCOMM Mobile Messaging Service for use with BlackBerry® smartphones. This gives ORBCOMM the opportunity to Push Content like information, alerts and device management directly to an Icon on the BlackBerry® smartphone home screen. The BlackBerry® Push Service is an essential component of the OMMS for use with BlackBerry® smartphonesreal-time, always-on experience, an incredibly efficient way of connecting the worlds assets always within reach. Pyat Level located in Waterloo, Ontario and a development office in Minsk, Belarus, has the knowledge and expertise to develop and deploy a wide range of Mobile Applications and Services tailored to telematics, to help leverage the Machine-2-Smartphone evolution.
